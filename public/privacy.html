Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2025

At Ask Mo Anything ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Email addresses when you sign up for our mobile app waitlist or contact us through our contact form.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including the questions you ask, pages you visit, and features you use.
  • Device Information: Information about your device, browser type, IP address, and operating system.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and collect information about how you use our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Notifying you about changes to our services
  • Providing customer support
  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve our services
  • Sending you emails regarding our mobile app launch and updates
  • Preventing fraudulent activities and enhancing security

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored in Firebase and may be processed through Formspree for contact form submissions. We use industry-standard security practices to safeguard your information, but no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as:

  • Firebase: For storing waitlist email addresses
  • Formspree: For processing contact form submissions
  • Vercel Analytics: For website usage analytics

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing their policies to understand how they process your data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal information we have about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to object to processing of your personal information
  • The right to data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us through our website contact form.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website contact form.
